# Jhin

Champion: Jhin

Class: Utility Marksman

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Medium

Primary Damage: Burst Physical

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Trading
- Target Selection
- Reload Management

---

# Champion Identity

Jhin is a burst-oriented marksman who excels at long-range picks, lane pressure, and setting up teammates. Unlike traditional ADCs, Jhin values precision over attack speed and wins fights through disciplined positioning, crowd control, and high-impact critical strikes.

---

# Strengths

- Strong lane trading.
- High burst damage.
- Excellent long-range engage with Curtain Call (R).
- Strong crowd control through Deadly Flourish (W).
- Powerful pick potential.

---

# Weaknesses

- Fixed attack speed.
- Vulnerable during reloads.
- Lower sustained DPS than hypercarries.
- Limited mobility.
- Punished heavily for poor positioning.

---

# Win Condition

Build an early advantage through lane pressure.

Control fights with long-range damage.

Use picks to create objective advantages.

Stay alive to deliver consistent critical strikes.

---

# Lose Condition

Getting caught before fights.

Reloading at the wrong time.

Using Ultimate in unsafe positions.

Trying to front-line against sustained DPS champions.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Punish enemy last hits.
- Trade around your fourth shot.
- Use Q to bounce through minions.
- Coordinate W with support crowd control.
- Push when safe to create lane priority.

---

# Mid Game

Rotate with your team.

Look for picks before objectives.

Use traps to control jungle entrances.

Play around vision.

---

# Late Game

Position safely behind teammates.

Use Curtain Call to start or finish fights.

Prioritize high-value targets when safe.

Manage reload timing carefully.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Fighting while reloading.
- Wasting the fourth shot on low-value targets.
- Using Ultimate too close to enemies.
- Chasing kills instead of maintaining safe positioning.
- Standing still during fights.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Fourth-shot usage.
- Reload management.
- Positioning.
- Crowd control follow-up.
- Objective participation.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Jhin players generally:

- Maximize fourth-shot value.
- Rarely die before objectives.
- Use W to extend teammate crowd control.
- Position safely while maintaining pressure.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Use every fourth shot on an enemy champion when possible.
- Stay alive through every major objective fight.
- Follow allied crowd control with W consistently.
- Position safely before channeling Curtain Call.
