# 07 — Monetization

Premium is designed around one feeling: **the member has hired a personal League
coach.** Not "more statistics unlocked." Every premium feature should save time,
personalize coaching, and improve as more games are analyzed.

## Premium Features

### Unlimited AI Coach
No caps on Quick Ask or coaching sessions.

### Daily Coaching
A fresh, personalized briefing each day based on recent games and the active
focus.

### Weekly Reports
A summary of the week: what improved, what recurred, and the focus for next week.

### Champion Mastery Tracking
Deep per-champion progression: grades, trends, best/weakest skills, and mastery
milestones.

### Replay Coaching
Coaching tied to specific replay moments ("Replay This Match", Challenger
Perspective).

### Matchup Library
Personalized matchup guidance (ADC vs ADC + support), tuned to the member's
tendencies.

### Goal Tracking
The goal engine tracks streaks toward the active focus and rotates focus on
success.

### Coaching Timeline
A chronological record of every coaching session and focus change.

### Progress History
Long-term graphs across last 5/10/25/50 games and skill trends.

### Premium Match Reports
Expanded per-match reports with full evidence, category grades, and coach's
notes.

## Member Experience Goal

Every session should leave a member feeling: "I know exactly what I improved, I
know exactly what is holding me back, and I know exactly what I'm focusing on
next."

## Free vs Premium (guideline)

- **Free:** limited coaching, recent match import, basic dashboard.
- **Premium:** unlimited coaching, daily/weekly reports, timeline, replay and
  matchup coaching, full history.
