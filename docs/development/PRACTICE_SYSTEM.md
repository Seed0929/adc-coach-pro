# BotDiff Practice System

Version: 1.0

Status: Core System

Priority: Critical

---

# Purpose

The Practice System transforms coaching recommendations into structured improvement plans.

Rather than simply identifying mistakes, BotDiff assigns personalized drills designed to improve the player's weakest skills through deliberate practice.

Every drill should be measurable, repeatable, and directly connected to Player DNA and current coaching goals.

---

# Philosophy

Knowledge alone does not improve gameplay.

Improvement requires:

Identify Weakness

↓

Assign Goal

↓

Practice Correct Skill

↓

Measure Progress

↓

Reevaluate

↓

Increase Difficulty

BotDiff should coach like a real performance coach—not simply provide statistics.

# Success Criteria

The Practice System succeeds when

Every coaching report ends with a practical next step.

Practice drills directly address identified weaknesses.

Drills adapt as the player improves.

Practice completion influences Player DNA and future coaching.

Players can clearly connect their practice to measurable in-game improvement.

The Practice System transforms coaching from passive feedback into an active training program.
