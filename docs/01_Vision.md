# 01 — Vision

## Mission

BotDiff exists to make every player improve faster than they ever could alone.

BotDiff is **not** a statistics website. Stat sites tell you *what* happened.
BotDiff tells you *why* it happened, *what pattern caused it*, and *exactly what
to change before your next ranked game*.

## What BotDiff Is

BotDiff is an **AI League of Legends coach** that learns how each individual
player plays over time. It builds a long-term model of a player's habits,
strengths, weaknesses, and improvement trends — and coaches those habits, not
isolated mistakes.

The north-star experience: **a member should feel like they hired a Challenger
coach** who has watched hundreds of their games and remembers every session.

## Coaching Philosophy

- One match can be misleading. Ten matches reveal habits. Fifty reveal identity.
- Coach recurring habits, not single-game noise.
- Fix the highest-impact leak first. One habit corrected beats twenty micro-tips.
- Every recommendation must be backed by evidence from the player's own games.
- Never generic. Advice that could apply to anyone is a failure.
- Always end with one measurable goal for the next game.

## The Three Outcomes

Every session should leave the player knowing:

1. What they improved.
2. What is holding them back.
3. What they are focusing on next.

## Long-Term Vision

BotDiff becomes the personal coach that grows smarter after every imported
match. It starts with ADC as the flagship role and expands to every role while
keeping the same memory-driven, evidence-based coaching engine at its core.
The end state: a coaching platform players trust more than a human coach because
it never forgets, never guesses, and is always available.
