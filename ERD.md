# BotDiff System Architecture

Version: 0.1
Status: MVP

---

# Purpose

This document describes the complete architecture of the BotDiff platform.

It explains how every major component communicates, how gameplay data flows through the system, and how AI coaching is generated.

This document serves as the technical blueprint for development.

---

# Mission

BotDiff is an AI-powered coaching platform for League of Legends ADC players.

Unlike traditional coaching platforms, BotDiff analyzes player-specific gameplay patterns to produce personalized coaching, measurable improvement plans, and long-term skill tracking.

---

# Core Design Principles

## Player First

Every feature should improve the player's learning experience.

---

## Data Driven

Every recommendation must be supported by measurable gameplay evidence.

---

## Personalized

The coaching engine adapts to the player's own habits instead of giving generic advice.

---

## Modular

Every subsystem should be replaceable without affecting the rest of the platform.

---

## Scalable

The architecture should support future additions including:

- Live coaching
- Multiple games
- Team analysis
- Mobile applications
- Desktop overlay

## Riot API Layer

Responsibilities

- Retrieve player information
- Download match history
- Retrieve timeline events
- Retrieve ranked information
- Retrieve champion mastery

---

## Match Processing Engine

Converts raw Riot timeline data into structured gameplay events.

Responsible for:

- Farming
- Deaths
- Teamfights
- Objective control
- Vision
- Trading
- Wave states
- Positioning

---

## Metrics Engine

Calculates every measurable performance metric.

Examples

- CS/min
- Gold efficiency
- Damage share
- Death efficiency
- Vision score
- Recall efficiency
- Objective participation
- Kill participation
- Positioning score
- Trading score

---

## Player Model

Stores long-term player information.

Includes

- Champion pool
- Rank history
- Preferred playstyle
- Mechanical score
- Macro score
- Farming score
- Vision score
- Aggression score
- Consistency
- Improvement trends

---

## Coaching Engine

Transforms metrics into coaching insights.

Produces

- Skill grades
- Weaknesses
- Strengths
- Improvement priorities
- Practice drills
- Personalized goals

---

## AI Layer

Uses OpenAI to transform structured coaching data into natural language reports.

The AI never invents gameplay information.

It only explains the data generated by BotDiff.

# Guiding Philosophy

Raw gameplay becomes structured events.

Structured events become metrics.

Metrics become coaching decisions.

Coaching decisions become personalized AI reports.

The architecture is designed so every layer has one responsibility and can evolve independently.
