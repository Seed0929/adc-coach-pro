# Smolder

Champion: Smolder

Class: Scaling Marksman

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Weak

Scaling: Elite

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Medium

Primary Damage: Physical with True Damage Scaling

Coach Priority:

- Farming
- Stack Progression
- Positioning
- Objective Timing

---

# Champion Identity

Smolder is a scaling marksman whose power grows throughout the game as he collects Dragon Practice stacks. Rather than forcing early fights, Smolder succeeds by farming efficiently, reaching important stack milestones on time, and becoming a dominant late-game carry.

Patience and consistency are more valuable than early kills.

---

# Strengths

- Excellent late-game scaling.
- Powerful execute damage.
- Safe farming tools.
- Strong objective damage.
- Reliable poke.
- High teamfight impact after stacking.

---

# Weaknesses

- Weak early game.
- Relies heavily on stack progression.
- Vulnerable before item and stack milestones.
- Limited mobility.
- Easily punished if behind.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach important Dragon Practice stack milestones.

Avoid unnecessary early fights.

Scale into a late-game teamfight carry.

---

# Lose Condition

Missing farm.

Delaying stack progression.

Taking unnecessary early deaths.

Fighting before scaling.

Ignoring objective timers.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize last-hitting.
- Stack Dragon Practice whenever possible.
- Trade only when safe.
- Maintain healthy wave states.
- Recall on favorable timings.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming side waves safely.

Rotate early for Dragons.

Avoid risky skirmishes.

Maintain stack progression while helping with objectives.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Stay behind your frontline.

Use your execute damage on priority targets when possible.

Prioritize survival over aggressive positioning.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Fighting too often before scaling.
- Falling behind in stacks.
- Roaming at the expense of farm.
- Standing too close to enemy engage.
- Chasing kills instead of maintaining DPS.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Dragon Practice stack progression.
- CS per minute.
- Positioning.
- Teamfight survival.
- Objective participation.
- Damage uptime.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Smolder players generally:

- Reach stack milestones on schedule.
- Maintain 8+ CS/min.
- Stay alive through major objective fights.
- Scale consistently into late game.
- Maximize damage while remaining safe.

---

# Power Spikes

Early Stack Milestones

- Improved trading and wave clear.

Level 6

- Ultimate adds significant teamfight impact.

One Item

- Farming becomes faster and more reliable.

Two Items

- Strong mid-game damage.

Late Stack Milestones

- Execute damage becomes a defining strength in teamfights.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Am I at an important stack milestone?
- Is my Ultimate available?
- Can I safely hit the closest target?
- Do I have enough space to kite?
- Is this fight worth delaying my farm?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Stay above 8 CS/min.
- Hit your Dragon Practice stack milestones consistently.
- Stay alive through every Dragon and Baron fight.
