# Senna

Champion: Senna

Class: Utility Marksman

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Average

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Average

Mobility: Low

Primary Damage: Physical

Coach Priority:

- Soul Collection
- Positioning
- Trading
- Team Utility

---

# Champion Identity

Senna is a scaling utility marksman who becomes stronger by collecting Mist through trading, farming, and champion interactions. Unlike traditional ADCs, she contributes through long-range damage, healing, shielding, and crowd control.

Senna wins games by steadily increasing her range, supporting teammates, and dealing safe, consistent damage from a distance.

---

# Strengths

- Infinite scaling through Mist.
- Exceptional attack range.
- Strong poke.
- Team healing and shielding.
- Long-range engage potential.
- Excellent late-game utility.

---

# Weaknesses

- Slow attack speed.
- Weak mobility.
- Vulnerable to assassins.
- Relatively weak wave clear.
- Punished heavily when caught.

---

# Win Condition

Collect Mist efficiently.

Farm safely.

Scale into long-range teamfights.

Support teammates while dealing consistent damage.

---

# Lose Condition

Ignoring Mist stacks.

Getting caught before fights.

Poor positioning.

Missing opportunities to trade safely.

Overextending without vision.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade to collect Mist.
- Farm safely.
- Respect engage supports.
- Use Piercing Darkness (Q) to heal allies and poke enemies.
- Push waves before recalling when safe.

---

# Mid Game

Rotate with teammates.

Collect Mist whenever possible.

Control vision around objectives.

Look for long-range picks with Last Embrace (W).

---

# Late Game

Stay behind your frontline.

Attack from maximum range.

Use Dawning Shadow (R) to influence fights anywhere on the map.

Balance damage with supporting teammates.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Ignoring free Mist stacks.
- Standing too close to enemy engage.
- Using Ultimate too late.
- Chasing kills.
- Forgetting to heal teammates.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Senna players generally:

- Collect Mist consistently throughout the game.
- Maintain safe positioning.
- Provide meaningful healing during fights.
- Land impactful roots.
- Stay alive through major objective fights.

---

# Power Spikes

Early Mist Stacks

- Increased range and trading power.

Level 6

- Dawning Shadow adds global impact.

One Item

- Reliable poke and utility.

Two Items

- Strong teamfight contribution.

Late Game

- Exceptional range and scaling become game-defining.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Am I positioned at maximum range?
- Can I safely collect more Mist?
- Is Dawning Shadow available?
- Which ally is most important to protect?
- Can I contribute without stepping into danger?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Collect Mist consistently throughout the game.
- Stay alive through every Dragon and Baron fight.
- Land at least one impactful global Ultimate.
- Heal teammates whenever possible during extended fights.
