# Lucian

Champion: Lucian

Class: Lane Bully Marksman

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Excellent

Scaling: Average

Teamfighting: Good

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Burst Physical

Coach Priority:

- Trading
- Positioning
- Ability Weaving
- Tempo

---

# Champion Identity

Lucian is an aggressive, early-game marksman who creates leads through constant pressure, short trades, and superior mobility. He rewards players who understand cooldown windows, spacing, and lane tempo.

Lucian wins games by snowballing early advantages before late-game hypercarries outscale him.

---

# Strengths

- Extremely strong early trading.
- High mobility with Relentless Pursuit (E).
- Excellent burst damage.
- Strong lane priority.
- Powerful skirmishing.
- Safe repositioning.

---

# Weaknesses

- Short attack range.
- Falls off compared to hypercarries.
- Requires mechanical precision.
- Punished heavily for failed aggression.
- Can struggle when behind.

---

# Win Condition

Create an early gold advantage.

Dominate lane through efficient trading.

Secure early objectives.

Snowball the game before enemy carries scale.

---

# Lose Condition

Passive laning.

Even farm against scaling champions.

Overextending without vision.

Wasting mobility aggressively.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade around passive double shots.
- Punish enemy cooldowns.
- Maintain lane priority.
- Crash waves before recalling.
- Coordinate aggressively with support.

---

# Mid Game

Look for skirmishes.

Rotate quickly after taking bot tower.

Pressure side lanes safely.

Control neutral objectives.

---

# Late Game

Stay with your team.

Use mobility defensively when necessary.

Focus the closest safe target.

Avoid isolated fights.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Dashing forward unnecessarily.
- Fighting into larger minion waves.
- Ignoring wave state.
- Overestimating burst damage.
- Falling behind in CS while forcing trades.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Lane dominance.
- Trading efficiency.
- Dash (E) usage.
- CS consistency.
- Death timing.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Lucian players generally:

- Build early CS and gold leads.
- Win most short trades.
- Use Relentless Pursuit to reposition rather than overcommit.
- Convert lane priority into objectives.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Build a CS lead by 10 minutes.
- Trade only when key enemy abilities are on cooldown.
- Save Relentless Pursuit unless it creates a clear advantage.
- Help secure the first Dragon after pushing the wave.
