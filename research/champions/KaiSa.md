# Kai'Sa

Champion: Kai'Sa

Class: Hybrid Hypercarry

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Average

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Mixed (Physical + Magic)

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Farming
- Target Selection
- Ultimate Usage

---

# Champion Identity

Kai'Sa is a flexible hypercarry who excels at cleaning up fights and diving vulnerable targets once key cooldowns have been used.

She is strongest when she reaches her item evolutions and plays patiently until the right opportunity appears.

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional late-game scaling.
- High mobility with Killer Instinct (R).
- Strong mixed damage.
- Excellent follow-up damage.
- Can adapt item builds to the game.

---

# Weaknesses

- Short attack range.
- Weak if behind.
- Vulnerable before evolutions.
- Can easily overcommit with Ultimate.
- Requires excellent positioning.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach item evolutions quickly.

Stay alive until enemy threats are used.

Use Ultimate aggressively only when safe.

---

# Lose Condition

Forcing early fights.

Using Ultimate into multiple enemies.

Poor farming delaying evolutions.

Greedy positioning.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Farm efficiently.
- Trade around passive stacks.
- Avoid unnecessary all-ins.
- Respect stronger early-game lanes.
- Manage waves for safe recalls.

---

# Mid Game

Look for isolated targets.

Join objective fights.

Play around teammates with crowd control.

Continue farming toward item spikes.

---

# Late Game

Fight patiently.

Attack the closest safe target first.

Use Ultimate to reposition, not just engage.

Clean up fights after key enemy abilities are gone.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Diving too early with Ultimate.
- Ignoring farm for skirmishes.
- Fighting before item evolutions.
- Overestimating burst damage.
- Chasing kills alone.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Evolution timing.
- Farming consistency.
- Ultimate decision making.
- Positioning.
- Damage uptime.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Kai'Sa players generally:

- Reach evolutions on time.
- Maintain high CS/min.
- Use Ultimate primarily as a repositioning tool.
- Stay alive until the end of teamfights.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 8 CS/min.
- Delay aggressive Ultimate usage until key enemy crowd control is unavailable.
- Reach first evolution as early as possible.
- Prioritize survival over risky assassinations.
