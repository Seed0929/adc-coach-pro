# Varus

Champion: Varus

Class: Versatile Marksman

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Very Low

Primary Damage: Physical (Hybrid Possible)

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Ultimate Usage
- Trading
- Ability Accuracy

---

# Champion Identity

Varus is a versatile marksman who excels at controlling fights through long-range poke, crowd control, and consistent damage. His build changes how he deals damage, but his success always depends on disciplined positioning and smart ability usage.

---

# Strengths

- Strong laning phase.
- Excellent poke.
- Reliable engage with Chain of Corruption (R).
- Flexible itemization.
- High teamfight impact.
- Strong objective control.

---

# Weaknesses

- No mobility.
- Easily punished by assassins.
- Requires accurate skillshots.
- Vulnerable if Ultimate is unavailable.
- Positioning mistakes are heavily punished.

---

# Win Condition

Create lane pressure.

Secure objective control through wave priority.

Land impactful Ultimates.

Maintain safe positioning while dealing consistent damage.

---

# Lose Condition

Missing key abilities.

Poor positioning.

Using Ultimate on low-value targets.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade with Piercing Arrow (Q).
- Punish enemy last hits.
- Maintain lane priority.
- Crash waves before recalling.
- Respect jungle pressure.

---

# Mid Game

Pressure objectives with poke.

Look for picks before fights.

Rotate with teammates.

Control vision around Dragons and Baron.

---

# Late Game

Stay behind the frontline.

Use Ultimate to begin fights or punish overextensions.

Attack the closest safe target.

Avoid greedy positioning.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Charging Q for too long.
- Using Ultimate without follow-up.
- Standing too close to enemy engage.
- Ignoring wave management.
- Chasing kills instead of securing objectives.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Ability accuracy.
- Positioning.
- Ultimate effectiveness.
- Objective participation.
- CS consistency.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Varus players generally:

- Maintain 7.5–8.5 CS/min.
- Consistently land impactful Ultimates.
- Deal damage safely throughout fights.
- Convert lane pressure into objectives.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 2

- Strong all-in potential with Blight stacks and abilities.

Level 6

- Chain of Corruption creates pick opportunities.

One Item

- Significant increase in poke or sustained damage.

Two Items

- Strong objective and teamfight presence.

Three Items

- Excellent sustained DPS while controlling fights.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Stay above 7.5 CS/min.
- Land at least one fight-winning Ultimate each game.
- Push waves before every Dragon.
- Prioritize survival over risky kills.
