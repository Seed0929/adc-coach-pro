# Vayne

Champion: Vayne

Class: Duelist Hypercarry

Difficulty: Very Hard

Lane Phase: Weak

Scaling: Elite

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Poor

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical & True Damage

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Target Selection
- Farming
- Tumble Discipline

---

# Champion Identity

Vayne is a late-game duelist who specializes in eliminating high-health targets through true damage while outplaying opponents with mobility and stealth.

She rarely wins through lane dominance. Instead, she succeeds by farming efficiently, surviving the early game, and carrying extended fights after reaching core items.

---

# Strengths

- Elite late-game scaling.
- Exceptional tank shredding.
- High outplay potential.
- Excellent dueling.
- Strong mobility with Tumble (Q).
- Powerful self-peel with Condemn (E).

---

# Weaknesses

- Weak early laning.
- Very short attack range.
- Poor wave clear.
- Difficult to play from behind.
- Punished heavily for positioning mistakes.

---

# Win Condition

Farm safely.

Reach two to three completed items.

Stay alive in teamfights.

Attack the closest target while maximizing Silver Bolts (W) procs.

---

# Lose Condition

Taking unnecessary early fights.

Falling behind in farm.

Using Tumble aggressively without reason.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize CS over trading.
- Respect stronger early-game lanes.
- Keep the wave close to your tower when possible.
- Trade only when advantageous.
- Recall on strong wave states.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming efficiently.

Avoid unnecessary skirmishes.

Join objective fights when strong enough.

Pressure side lanes only with vision.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Use Final Hour (R) before extended fights.

Use Tumble to reposition, not to engage.

Focus the closest safe target.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Tumbling forward unnecessarily.
- Chasing carries through enemy frontlines.
- Ignoring farm for risky fights.
- Misusing Condemn.
- Fighting before item spikes.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Positioning.
- Tumble usage.
- Farming consistency.
- Teamfight survival.
- Target selection.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Vayne players generally:

- Maintain 7.5–8.5 CS/min.
- Rarely die before major objectives.
- Use Tumble primarily for repositioning.
- Maximize Silver Bolts uptime.
- Survive until the end of teamfights.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 6

- Final Hour unlocks significant dueling and outplay potential.

One Item

- Reliable dueling begins.

Two Items

- Major sustained damage increase.

Three Items

- Elite carry capable of deciding games through positioning and DPS.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Which enemy crowd control ability can kill me?
- Do I have Final Hour available?
- Can I safely attack the nearest target?
- Is Tumble available to reposition?
- Am I fighting before my item spike?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Never Tumble forward unless it secures a safe kill.
- Stay alive through every Dragon and Baron fight.
- Focus the closest safe target in every teamfight.
