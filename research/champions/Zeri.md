# Zeri

Champion: Zeri

Class: Mobile Hypercarry

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Weak

Scaling: Elite

Teamfighting: Elite

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Very High

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Kiting
- Farming
- Ultimate Usage

---

# Champion Identity

Zeri is a late-game hypercarry who excels in extended fights through exceptional mobility and sustained damage. She rewards disciplined positioning, efficient farming, and smart use of mobility to outlast opponents rather than burst them down.

Zeri wins games by reaching her item spikes, surviving the early game, and taking over long teamfights.

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional mobility.
- Elite late-game scaling.
- Excellent sustained DPS.
- Difficult to lock down.
- Strong cleanup potential.
- Excels in extended fights.

---

# Weaknesses

- Weak early lane.
- Item dependent.
- Short effective range.
- Punished heavily when behind.
- Requires excellent mechanics.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach two to three completed items.

Survive the early game.

Use mobility to stay alive while dealing continuous damage.

---

# Lose Condition

Taking unnecessary early fights.

Falling behind in CS.

Overusing mobility aggressively.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize farming over risky trades.
- Respect stronger early-game ADCs.
- Keep the wave in a safe position.
- Recall on good wave timings.
- Avoid unnecessary deaths.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming efficiently.

Join objective fights on time.

Avoid isolated skirmishes.

Look for extended fights where mobility provides value.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Keep moving constantly.

Use Ultimate before committing to long fights.

Attack the closest safe target.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Fighting before item spikes.
- Dashing into danger.
- Chasing kills too aggressively.
- Missing farm while roaming.
- Standing still during fights.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- CS per minute.
- Positioning.
- Ultimate timing.
- Fight duration.
- Death timing.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Zeri players generally:

- Maintain 8+ CS/min.
- Rarely die before major objectives.
- Maximize damage in long fights.
- Use mobility primarily to reposition safely.
- Scale consistently into late game.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 6

- Ultimate provides significant teamfight power.

One Item

- Farming becomes faster and fights become safer.

Two Items

- Strong sustained damage.

Three Items

- Elite carry potential capable of taking over teamfights.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Have I reached my item spike?
- Is my Ultimate available?
- Which enemy ability can stop my movement?
- Can I safely kite backward?
- Am I attacking the closest safe target?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 8 CS/min.
- Avoid dying before 15 minutes.
- Use Ultimate before committing to major teamfights.
- Stay alive until the end of every objective fight.
