# Miss Fortune

Champion: Miss Fortune

Class: Lane Bully Marksman

Difficulty: Easy

Lane Phase: Excellent

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Excellent

Mobility: Medium

Primary Damage: Physical Burst & Sustained DPS

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Ultimate Usage
- Lane Pressure
- Objective Setup

---

# Champion Identity

Miss Fortune is a dominant laning marksman who excels at controlling waves, punishing poor positioning, and winning teamfights with well-timed Bullet Time (R).

She creates advantages through strong early trading and converts them into dragons, towers, and decisive objective fights.

---

# Strengths

- Excellent lane bullying.
- Strong wave clear.
- Powerful early game.
- High teamfight impact.
- Outstanding objective control.
- Easy to learn but difficult to master.

---

# Weaknesses

- No true mobility.
- Easily interrupted during Ultimate.
- Falls behind if early pressure is wasted.
- Vulnerable to assassins and hard engage.
- Requires excellent positioning before fights.

---

# Win Condition

Win lane through consistent trading.

Secure lane priority.

Control Dragons with wave advantage.

Use Bullet Time to decide major teamfights.

---

# Lose Condition

Even lane against scaling carries.

Poor Ultimate positioning.

Overextending without vision.

Using Bullet Time where it can be easily interrupted.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade with Double Up (Q) through low-health minions.
- Maintain wave control.
- Push when safe to gain priority.
- Punish enemy recalls.
- Respect jungle pressure.

---

# Mid Game

Rotate after taking bot tower.

Push waves before objectives.

Look for Ultimate opportunities in choke points.

Avoid isolated fights.

---

# Late Game

Stay behind your frontline.

Wait patiently for the right Ultimate.

Prioritize surviving over forcing damage.

Follow up on allied crowd control.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Channeling Bullet Time too early.
- Using Ultimate where enemies can easily escape.
- Walking too far forward before fights.
- Ignoring wave management.
- Fighting without lane priority.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Lane pressure.
- Wave control.
- Ultimate effectiveness.
- Positioning.
- Objective participation.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Miss Fortune players generally:

- Create early CS and gold leads.
- Consistently gain lane priority.
- Use Bullet Time to influence objectives.
- Stay alive through major teamfights.
- Convert lane wins into towers and Dragons.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Push the wave before every Dragon spawn.
- Use Bullet Time only after key enemy crowd control has been used.
- Stay alive through every major objective fight.
