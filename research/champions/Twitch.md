# Twitch

Champion: Twitch

Class: Ambush Hypercarry

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Weak

Scaling: Elite

Teamfighting: Elite

Wave Clear: Average

Mobility: Low

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Farming
- Positioning
- Ambush Timing
- Teamfighting

---

# Champion Identity

Twitch is a late-game hypercarry who thrives on surprise, positioning, and extended teamfights. Rather than winning through lane dominance, Twitch creates advantages by farming efficiently, choosing smart ambush opportunities, and dealing massive area-of-effect damage once he reaches his core items.

Patience is Twitch's greatest strength.

---

# Strengths

- Elite late-game scaling.
- Massive teamfight damage.
- Excellent ambush potential.
- Strong objective damage.
- Can punish poor vision.
- Powerful cleanup potential.

---

# Weaknesses

- Weak early laning.
- No true mobility.
- Easily punished when revealed.
- Relies heavily on positioning.
- Vulnerable before item spikes.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach two to three completed items.

Look for high-value ambushes.

Stay alive during teamfights while maximizing Spray and Pray (R).

---

# Lose Condition

Forcing early fights.

Falling behind in farm.

Opening fights from poor positions.

Overextending after leaving stealth.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize CS over risky trades.
- Respect stronger early-game lanes.
- Keep the wave in a safe position.
- Trade only when advantageous.
- Recall on strong wave states.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming efficiently.

Use Ambush (Q) to create picks when safe.

Avoid unnecessary skirmishes.

Rotate early for Dragons and Herald.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Activate Spray and Pray before committing.

Position to hit multiple enemies.

Attack the closest safe target while maximizing piercing autos.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Leaving stealth without a clear plan.
- Fighting before item spikes.
- Chasing kills too aggressively.
- Flanking alone.
- Ignoring vision before ambushing.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- CS per minute.
- Positioning.
- Ambush timing.
- Teamfight survival.
- Objective participation.
- Damage uptime.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Twitch players generally:

- Maintain 8+ CS/min.
- Reach late game with few unnecessary deaths.
- Position to hit multiple enemies with Ultimate.
- Choose ambushes with numerical advantages.
- Convert successful fights into objectives.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 6

- Spray and Pray unlocks major teamfight potential.

One Item

- Reliable sustained damage.

Two Items

- Strong carry potential.

Three Items

- Elite hypercarry capable of deciding games through positioning.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Am I entering from an unexpected angle?
- Is Spray and Pray available?
- Can I hit multiple enemies?
- Which enemy champion can immediately threaten me?
- Can my team follow up if I reveal from stealth?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 8 CS/min.
- Avoid dying before your second completed item.
- Use Ambush to create advantages, not random fights.
- Position to hit at least two champions with Spray and Pray during every major teamfight.
