# Tristana

Champion: Tristana

Class: Snowball Hypercarry

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Excellent

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Jump Decision Making
- Snowball Conversion
- Positioning
- Objective Pressure

---

# Champion Identity

Tristana is a marksman who combines explosive early-game all-ins with exceptional late-game scaling. She creates advantages through aggressive trading, tower pressure, and safe repositioning with Rocket Jump (W).

A successful Tristana player knows when to play aggressively and when to transition into a disciplined late-game carry.

---

# Strengths

- Elite tower damage.
- Strong level 2 all-ins.
- Excellent self-peel with Buster Shot (R).
- High mobility.
- Outstanding late-game scaling.
- Strong objective pressure.

---

# Weaknesses

- Can overcommit with Rocket Jump.
- Vulnerable when Jump is unavailable.
- Requires good target selection.
- Easily punished for failed engages.
- Short attack range early.

---

# Win Condition

Create an early lead through strong trading.

Take tower plates.

Secure the first bot turret.

Transition into a safe late-game carry.

---

# Lose Condition

Jumping into unwinnable fights.

Ignoring wave management.

Throwing shutdowns after getting ahead.

Overchasing kills.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Look for level 2 all-ins.
- Trade around Explosive Charge (E).
- Push when safe.
- Punish enemy recalls.
- Respect jungle pressure.

---

# Mid Game

Pressure side lanes.

Destroy towers.

Rotate quickly after taking objectives.

Join fights around Dragons and Herald.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Use Rocket Jump primarily to reposition or reset after kills.

Attack the closest safe target.

Use Buster Shot to peel dangerous threats.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Rocket Jumping into multiple enemies.
- Diving towers without vision.
- Fighting without cooldowns.
- Ignoring objectives after winning lane.
- Using Buster Shot offensively when peel is needed.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Rocket Jump usage.
- Tower damage.
- Objective conversion.
- Positioning.
- Death timing.
- Teamfight survival.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Tristana players generally:

- Build early gold leads.
- Secure first tower frequently.
- Use Rocket Jump resets effectively.
- Stay alive during major objective fights.
- Convert lane leads into map pressure.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 2

- Elite all-in potential.

Level 6

- Buster Shot provides strong kill pressure and self-peel.

One Item

- Excellent skirmishing and tower damage.

Two Items

- Major carry potential.

Three Items

- Elite late-game hypercarry.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Can I save Rocket Jump for escape or a reset?
- Which enemy champion can immediately punish my jump?
- Is Buster Shot better used offensively or defensively?
- Can we secure an objective after this fight?
- Am I attacking the closest safe target?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Win the level 2 race in lane.
- Take the first bot tower whenever you gain an advantage.
- Never Rocket Jump into multiple enemies without follow-up.
- Convert every won fight into a tower, Dragon, or Baron.
