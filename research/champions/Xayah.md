# Xayah

Champion: Xayah

Class: Self-Peel Hypercarry

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Good

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Excellent

Mobility: Medium

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Feather Management
- Ultimate Usage
- Teamfighting

---

# Champion Identity

Xayah is a scaling marksman who excels at punishing enemies that dive too aggressively. She controls space with feathers, protects herself using Featherstorm (R), and deals massive sustained damage in extended fights.

She wins games by surviving engages and turning enemy aggression into favorable fights.

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional self-peel.
- Excellent teamfighting.
- High sustained DPS.
- Strong wave clear.
- Powerful anti-dive tools.
- Reliable objective damage.

---

# Weaknesses

- Shorter attack range than many ADCs.
- Requires good feather placement.
- Vulnerable when Ultimate is unavailable.
- Can struggle against heavy poke.
- Relies on positioning for maximum damage.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach core item spikes.

Use Featherstorm to avoid key enemy abilities.

Control fights through feather placement.

---

# Lose Condition

Using Featherstorm too early.

Poor feather positioning.

Getting caught before objectives.

Fighting without Ultimate against heavy engage.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Farm consistently.
- Trade around Double Daggers (Q) and feather placement.
- Push waves before recalling.
- Respect jungle pressure.
- Avoid unnecessary all-ins without cooldowns.

---

# Mid Game

Rotate after pushing waves.

Prepare feathers before committing.

Fight around objectives.

Stay near teammates.

---

# Late Game

Attack the closest safe target.

Use Featherstorm to dodge major engage abilities.

Recall feathers through multiple enemies whenever possible.

Prioritize survival over risky kills.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Panicking with Featherstorm.
- Forgetting feather positioning.
- Chasing kills away from feathers.
- Fighting alone.
- Using Ultimate offensively without an escape plan.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Featherstorm timing.
- Feather recall value.
- Positioning.
- Teamfight survival.
- CS consistency.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Xayah players generally:

- Save Featherstorm for key threats.
- Consistently hit multiple enemies with Bladecaller (E).
- Maintain 7.5–8.5 CS/min.
- Survive most late-game teamfights.
- Convert objective fights into victories.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 2

- Strong early trades with Double Daggers and Bladecaller.

Level 6

- Featherstorm provides powerful outplay potential.

One Item

- Reliable sustained damage.

Two Items

- Significant teamfight power.

Three Items

- Elite late-game carry potential.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Which enemy engage ability must I dodge with Featherstorm?
- Where are my feathers positioned?
- Can I hit multiple enemies with Bladecaller?
- Is Flash available?
- Can I safely attack the closest target?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Use Featherstorm to dodge at least one major ability each game.
- Hit multiple champions with Bladecaller whenever possible.
- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Stay alive through every Dragon and Baron fight.
