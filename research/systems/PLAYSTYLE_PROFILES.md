# Playstyle Profiles

Version: MVP v1

Purpose:

Identify recurring player habits over multiple games to personalize coaching.

BotDiff should classify a player's tendencies and adapt feedback accordingly.

---

# Philosophy

Players don't improve by hearing the same advice every game.

BotDiff should recognize patterns across multiple matches and coach the player based on their overall playstyle.

---

# Aggressive Player

Characteristics

- High kill participation
- High damage dealt
- High deaths
- Frequent early fights
- Low average game length

Strengths

- Creates pressure
- Confident mechanically
- Strong laning

Weaknesses

- Overforces fights
- Gives shutdowns
- Inconsistent leads

Coach Focus

Decision making

Fight selection

Risk management

---

# Passive Player

Characteristics

- Low deaths
- Low kill participation
- High farm
- Rarely initiates fights

Strengths

- Consistent economy
- Reliable scaling

Weaknesses

- Missed opportunities
- Low game impact

Coach Focus

Confidence

Punishing enemy mistakes

Joining fights earlier

---

# Farming Specialist

Characteristics

- Excellent CS
- High gold
- Low objective participation

Strengths

- Strong economy

Weaknesses

- Doesn't convert gold into wins

Coach Focus

Objective timing

Rotations

Map impact

---

# Teamfighter

Characteristics

- High KP
- High damage
- Good positioning

Strengths

- Strong in coordinated fights

Weaknesses

- May neglect side waves
- May lose tempo

Coach Focus

Wave management

Objective setup

---

# Split Pusher

Characteristics

- High side lane farm
- Low teamfight participation
- High tower damage

Strengths

- Creates pressure

Weaknesses

- Misses important objectives

Coach Focus

Recall timing

Teleport/map awareness

Objective grouping

---

# Coin Flip Player

Characteristics

- Very high kills
- Very high deaths
- Large game-to-game variance

Strengths

- Can carry games

Weaknesses

- Inconsistent

Coach Focus

Consistency

Decision making

Reducing unnecessary deaths

---

# Consistent Climber

Characteristics

- Stable CS
- Low deaths
- Strong objective participation
- Consistent grades

Strengths

- Reliable improvement

Weaknesses

- May lack carry confidence

Coach Focus

Advanced optimization

Champion mastery

Macro refinement

---

# Adaptation Rules

BotDiff should:

- Detect the player's profile after 10+ games.
- Update the profile as habits change.
- Personalize coaching based on the current profile.
- Avoid giving contradictory advice.

---

# MVP Rule

The player should feel that BotDiff understands how they play, not just how they performed in one match.
