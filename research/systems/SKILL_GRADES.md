# Skill Grades

Version: MVP v1

Purpose:

Define the objective standards used to assign grades for every coaching category.

Every category is graded independently before contributing to the Overall Grade.

---

# Grade Scale

S+

Exceptional

S

Outstanding

A

Very Good

B

Good

C

Average

D

Below Average

F

Critical Weakness

---

# Farming

Measures

- CS/min
- Gold/min
- Missed waves
- Recall timing
- Lane efficiency

Example Thresholds

S+

9.5+ CS/min

S

9.0–9.4

A

8.0–8.9

B

7.0–7.9

C

6.0–6.9

D

5.0–5.9

F

Below 5.0

---

# Positioning

Measures

- Death location
- Fight spacing
- Frontline distance
- Safety
- Escape usage

S+

Almost never dies first.

Maintains excellent spacing.

Always attacks safely.

F

Frequently dies first.

Walks into enemy engage.

Loses fights before dealing damage.

---

# Trading

Measures

- Lane trades
- Health advantage
- Cooldown usage
- Auto attack efficiency

S+

Consistently wins trades.

Creates lane pressure.

F

Loses nearly every trade.

Takes unnecessary damage.

---

# Teamfighting

Measures

- Damage uptime
- Target selection
- Survival
- Ability timing

S+

Excellent positioning.

High damage.

Rarely dies early.

F

Dies immediately.

Poor target selection.

Little impact.

---

# Macro

Measures

- Rotations
- Side lane assignments
- Tempo
- Wave management
- Objective timing

S+

Always in the correct place.

Excellent map decisions.

F

Frequently late.

Misses objectives.

Poor rotations.

---

# Objectives

Measures

- Dragon participation
- Baron participation
- Tower damage
- Objective conversion

S+

Creates advantages after every successful fight.

F

Rarely contributes to objectives.

Wins fights without gaining map control.

---

# Vision

Measures

- Wards placed
- Wards cleared
- Objective vision
- Face checks

S+

Excellent vision before objectives.

F

Rarely wards.

Frequently dies in fog of war.

---

# Death Timing

Measures

- Deaths before objectives
- Greedy deaths
- Isolated deaths
- Shutdown deaths

S+

Deaths are rare and low impact.

F

Repeated deaths before Dragons, Barons, or major fights.

---

# Improvement Philosophy

BotDiff does not coach every low grade.

Instead it identifies:

- Biggest weakness
- Second biggest weakness
- Biggest strength

Only one improvement becomes the primary coaching focus.

---

# Example Report

Overall Grade

B

Category Grades

Farming: A

Positioning: B

Macro: C

Vision: B

Objectives: C

Teamfighting: A

Primary Focus

Macro

Reason

Improving your map rotations will increase your impact more than improving mechanics.

Practice Goal

Arrive at every Dragon 30 seconds before it spawns.

# MVP Principles

Grades should be:

Consistent

Evidence-based

Easy to understand

Focused on improvement, not punishment
