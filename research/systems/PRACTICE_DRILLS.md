# Practice Drills

Version: MVP v1

Purpose:

Convert coaching recommendations into measurable, repeatable practice goals.

Every primary weakness identified by BotDiff should result in exactly one practice drill.

---

# Philosophy

Analysis without action does not create improvement.

Every report should end with one simple drill that the player can focus on for their next few games.

BotDiff should recommend drills that are:

- Measurable
- Simple
- Repeatable
- Relevant to the player's biggest weakness

Never assign more than one primary drill.

---

# Farming Drills

## Low CS

Goal

Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.

Success

Achieve this in 3 consecutive games.

---

## Missed Side Waves

Goal

Catch every safe side wave after 15 minutes.

Success

Miss fewer than 3 side waves.

---

## Poor Recall Timing

Goal

Recall only after crashing a wave whenever possible.

Success

Avoid recalling with a large allied wave pushing toward the enemy.

---

# Positioning Drills

## Dying First

Goal

Never be the first player to die in a teamfight.

Success

Complete 5 consecutive teamfights without being first death.

---

## Standing Too Close

Goal

Always attack from maximum safe range.

Success

Reduce unnecessary deaths caused by overextending.

---

## Chasing Kills

Goal

Stop attacking once the fight is already won.

Success

Reduce deaths occurring after winning fights.

---

# Teamfighting Drills

## Poor Target Selection

Goal

Attack the closest safe target.

Success

Maintain consistent damage throughout fights.

---

## Low Damage Uptime

Goal

Stay alive until the end of every major teamfight.

Success

Participate in at least 80% of your team's total fight duration.

---

# Macro Drills

## Late Rotations

Goal

Arrive at Dragon 30 seconds before spawn.

Success

Be present before every Dragon fight.

---

## Poor Wave Management

Goal

Push your wave before rotating.

Success

Avoid leaving large waves unattended.

---

## Weak Objective Conversion

Goal

After every won fight, immediately look for:

- Tower
- Dragon
- Baron
- Herald

Success

Convert at least half of winning fights into objectives.

---

# Vision Drills

## Low Vision Score

Goal

Place one Control Ward before every Dragon.

Success

Maintain consistent objective vision.

---

## Dangerous Face Checks

Goal

Never enter unwarded jungle alone.

Success

Reduce deaths in fog of war.

# Progression Rules

Easy drills

Single-game habits.

Intermediate drills

Complete over 3 consecutive games.

Advanced drills

Track improvement over 10 games.

---

# Completion Criteria

A drill is complete when:

- The measurable goal is achieved consistently.
- The associated skill grade improves.
- The weakness is no longer the player's highest priority.

Completed drills should be replaced with the next highest-impact improvement.

---

# MVP Rule

Each coaching report should end with:

Primary Focus

↓

One Practice Drill

↓

One Success Metric

↓

Estimated Completion (3–5 games)
