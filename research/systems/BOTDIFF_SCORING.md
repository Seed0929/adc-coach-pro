# BotDiff Scoring System

Version: MVP v1

Purpose:

Convert replay data into objective performance grades that identify the player's biggest opportunities for improvement.

---

# Philosophy

BotDiff does not reward flashy plays.

BotDiff rewards consistency.

Winning one fight does not outweigh poor farming, positioning, or repeated mistakes.

The goal is to measure habits that consistently lead to climbing.

---

# Overall Grade Scale

S+ Exceptional

S Outstanding

A Above Average

B Good

C Average

D Needs Improvement

F Critical Weakness

---

# Scoring Categories

## Farming

Weight: 25%

Measures

- CS/min
- Gold/min
- Missed waves
- Recall timing
- Lane economy

---

## Positioning

Weight: 20%

Measures

- Death location
- Distance from teammates
- Fight positioning
- Safe auto spacing
- Frontline usage

---

## Teamfighting

Weight: 15%

Measures

- Damage uptime
- Target selection
- Fight survival
- Kill participation
- Ability usage

---

## Macro

Weight: 15%

Measures

- Rotations
- Side lane management
- Wave assignments
- Tempo
- Objective setup

---

## Objectives

Weight: 10%

Measures

- Dragon participation
- Baron participation
- Tower damage
- Herald participation
- Objective conversion

---

## Trading

Weight: 10%

Measures

- Lane trades
- Health advantages
- Trade efficiency
- Ability accuracy

---

## Vision

Weight: 5%

Measures

- Wards placed
- Wards cleared
- Vision before objectives
- Dangerous face checks

# Priority Rules

BotDiff never overwhelms the player.

Every review should identify:

1 Primary Weakness

2 Supporting Issues

3 Positive Habits

---

Example

Primary

Poor Farming

Supporting

Late Rotations

Unsafe Positioning

Positive

Good Teamfight Target Selection

---

# Coaching Priority Order

When multiple weaknesses exist, prioritize them in this order.

1. Farming

2. Positioning

3. Death Timing

4. Objective Play

5. Teamfighting

6. Trading

7. Vision

---

# Champion Adjustments

Scores should be adjusted based on champion identity.

Examples

Draven

Increase weight on:

- Early Gold
- Lane Pressure
- Passive Cash-ins

Decrease weight on:

- Late Game Scaling

---

Jinx

Increase weight on:

- Farming
- Teamfighting
- Positioning

Decrease weight on:

- Early Aggression

---

Samira

Increase weight on:

- Engage Timing
- Ultimate Value

Decrease weight on:

- Early CS Advantage

---

Ziggs

Increase weight on:

- Tower Damage
- Wave Management

Decrease weight on:

- Auto Attack Damage

# Report Format

Every coaching report should contain:

Top 3 Improvements

Biggest Strength

Practice Goal

One Thing To Focus On Next Game

---

# MVP Rule

BotDiff should never recommend more than ONE primary improvement per match.

Players improve faster when focusing on one habit at a time.
