# BotDiff Metrics

Version: MVP v1

Purpose:

Define every gameplay metric BotDiff can analyze and explain how each metric contributes to coaching decisions.

---

# Economy

## CS per Minute

Measures farming efficiency.

Higher CS generally leads to earlier item spikes.

Target

ADC:
8–10 CS/min

---

## Gold per Minute

Measures overall economy.

Includes kills, farm, objectives and passive income.

---

## XP per Minute

Measures experience efficiency.

Higher XP often indicates better wave management and rotations.

---

# Combat

## KDA

Measures kills, deaths and assists.

Should never be used alone.

BotDiff values impact over KDA.

---

## Kill Participation

Measures involvement in team kills.

Low KP may indicate poor rotations.

High KP with many deaths may indicate overaggression.

---

## Damage Per Minute

Measures consistent damage output.

Should be compared against champion expectations.

---

## Damage Share

Percentage of team damage dealt.

Useful for identifying carry performance.

---

## Damage Taken

Measures frontline exposure.

High values on ADCs may indicate positioning problems.

---

# Objectives

## Dragon Participation

Participation in Dragons.

---

## Baron Participation

Participation in Baron fights.

---

## Herald Participation

Participation in Rift Herald.

---

## Tower Damage

Measures pressure on structures.

Especially important for:

- Caitlyn
- Tristana
- Ziggs

---

# Vision

## Vision Score

Overall contribution to map vision.

---

## Control Wards Purchased

Measures objective preparation.

---

## Wards Cleared

Measures vision denial.

---

# Survival

## Death Count

Total deaths.

BotDiff values meaningful deaths over raw count.

---

## First Death Rate

How often the player dies first in fights.

Important positioning metric.

---

## Shutdown Deaths

Deaths while holding shutdown gold.

These often have high game impact.

---

# Teamfighting

## Damage Uptime

Time spent dealing damage during fights.

---

## Fight Duration

Average survival during teamfights.

---

## Target Selection

Measures whether the player attacks safe, high-value targets.

---

# Laning

## Gold Difference @10

Measures lane advantage.

---

## XP Difference @10

Measures lane control.

---

## CS Difference @10

Measures farming advantage.

---

## Plates Earned

Measures lane pressure.

---

# Macro

## Side Wave Collection

Measures efficiency after lane phase.

---

## Objective Conversion

Winning fights into:

- Dragons
- Towers
- Baron
- Herald

---

## Recall Efficiency

Recalling on good wave states.

---

## Tempo

How efficiently the player moves around the map without wasting time.

---

# Champion Metrics

Some champions require additional metrics.

Examples

Draven

- Passive cash-ins
- Early gold lead

Smolder

- Dragon Practice stacks

Senna

- Mist collected

Kalista

- Objective secure rate

Jinx

- Reset participation

Ziggs

- Tower execution

---

# MVP Rule

BotDiff should only analyze metrics that lead to actionable coaching.

If a metric cannot produce meaningful advice, it should not influence the final report.
