# Caitlyn vs Jinx

Difficulty

Slightly Caitlyn Favored

---

# Lane Identity

Caitlyn wants to pressure early.

Jinx wants to survive and scale.

The lane is decided by wave control and health advantages more than all-ins.

---

# Caitlyn Win Condition

- Maintain permanent push.
- Abuse range advantage.
- Zone Jinx from CS.
- Chip tower plates.
- Secure early Dragons.

---

# Jinx Win Condition

- Minimize losses.
- Farm safely.
- Avoid unnecessary trades.
- Reach two items.
- Win later teamfights.

---

# Early Levels (1-3)

Caitlyn should:

- Auto every time Jinx walks up.
- Push for Level 2.
- Use traps aggressively.

Jinx should:

- Give up unsafe minions.
- Farm with rockets when necessary.
- Stay healthy.

---

# Levels 4-6

Caitlyn

- Continue wave pressure.
- Force awkward recalls.
- Punish bad positioning.

Jinx

- Farm under tower.
- Avoid all-ins.
- Track jungle pressure.

---

# Teamfights

Caitlyn

- Look for picks.
- Fight from range.
- Maintain spacing.

Jinx

- Protect positioning.
- Wait for resets.
- Front-to-back fights.

---

# Biggest Mistakes

Caitlyn

- Overextending for plates.
- Ignoring jungle pressure.
- Losing wave control.

Jinx

- Fighting early.
- Contesting every minion.
- Walking into trap setups.

---

# Coaching Triggers

Coach Caitlyn on:

- Lane pressure.
- Plate conversion.
- CS denial.

Coach Jinx on:

- Safe farming.
- Positioning.
- Scaling discipline
