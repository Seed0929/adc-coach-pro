# BotDiff Goal Engine

Version: 1.0

Status: Core System

Priority: Critical

---

# Purpose

The Goal Engine transforms coaching insights into measurable, actionable improvement objectives.

Instead of overwhelming players with dozens of mistakes, BotDiff identifies the highest-impact areas for improvement and creates personalized goals that evolve alongside the player's growth.

The Goal Engine bridges the gap between analysis and real improvement.

---

# Philosophy

Players improve through focused repetition.

BotDiff should never assign ten goals.

Instead, every player should always have:

• One Primary Goal

• One Secondary Goal

• One Long-Term Focus

The objective is steady improvement through deliberate practice.

Completed goals lead to new coaching priorities.

Goals evolve as Player DNA evolves.

The Goal Engine transforms coaching into continuous, measurable improvement rather than isolated match feedback.
