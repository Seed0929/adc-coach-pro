# Vel'Koz

Champion: Vel'Koz

Class: Artillery Mage

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Elite

Mobility: Very Low

Primary Damage: Magic Burst

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Skillshot Accuracy
- Wave Management
- Objective Control

---

# Champion Identity

Vel'Koz is a long-range artillery mage who dominates bot lane through superior poke, wave control, and true damage. He excels at wearing opponents down before fights begin and controlling space around objectives.

Vel'Koz wins games by maintaining safe positioning, landing consistent skillshots, and dealing massive damage from the backline.

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional poke.
- Elite wave clear.
- High burst damage.
- Long-range teamfight impact.
- Strong objective control.
- Excellent scaling.

---

# Weaknesses

- No mobility.
- Extremely vulnerable to assassins.
- Highly skillshot dependent.
- Weak when caught out of position.
- Requires frontline protection.

---

# Win Condition

Control lane through poke.

Maintain wave priority.

Force enemies to fight at low health.

Position safely during teamfights while maximizing true damage.

---

# Lose Condition

Missing important skillshots.

Getting caught before fights.

Ignoring wave management.

Standing within engage range.

Using Ultimate without protection.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Push waves efficiently.
- Harass with Plasma Fission (Q).
- Punish enemy last hits.
- Respect engage supports.
- Crash waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Control vision around objectives.

Poke before Dragons and Baron.

Rotate with wave priority.

Avoid isolated fights.

---

# Late Game

Stay behind your frontline.

Look for grouped enemies.

Use Life Form Disintegration Ray (R) after enemy crowd control has been used.

Prioritize survival over risky positioning.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Standing too close.
- Channeling Ultimate where it can easily be interrupted.
- Wasting abilities on tanks before objectives.
- Ignoring wave states.
- Chasing kills.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Skillshot accuracy.
- Positioning.
- Wave management.
- Ultimate effectiveness.
- Objective participation.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Vel'Koz players generally:

- Maintain high CS.
- Land consistent poke before objectives.
- Stay alive through major fights.
- Maximize true damage passive.
- Control space around Dragons and Baron.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight, ask:

- Am I outside enemy engage range?
- Which enemy can interrupt my Ultimate?
- Can I poke before the fight starts?
- Is my frontline in position?
- Can we secure an objective after winning this fight?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Land poke before every Dragon and Baron fight.
- Stay alive through every major objective.
- Use Ultimate only when protected by teammates.
