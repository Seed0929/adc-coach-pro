# Ziggs

Champion: Ziggs

Class: Artillery Mage

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Elite

Mobility: Low

Primary Damage: Magic Burst

Coach Priority:

- Wave Management
- Ability Accuracy
- Siege
- Objective Control

---

# Champion Identity

Ziggs is an artillery mage who dominates bot lane through exceptional wave clear, long-range poke, and unmatched tower destruction. Rather than relying on sustained auto attacks, Ziggs wins by controlling space, forcing enemies off objectives, and rapidly converting advantages into structures.

---

# Strengths

- Elite wave clear.
- Exceptional tower damage.
- Long-range poke.
- Excellent objective control.
- Safe farming.
- Strong late-game siege.

---

# Weaknesses

- No reliable mobility.
- Vulnerable to assassins.
- Skillshot dependent.
- Struggles if caught out.
- Can be punished by hard engage.

---

# Win Condition

Push waves constantly.

Control lane priority.

Destroy towers early.

Siege objectives safely.

Deal consistent poke before fights begin.

---

# Lose Condition

Missing key abilities.

Poor positioning.

Fighting in close quarters.

Ignoring objective timers.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Push waves efficiently.
- Harass with Bouncing Bomb (Q).
- Maintain lane priority.
- Punish enemy recalls.
- Save Satchel Charge (W) for tower executes or self-peel when appropriate.

---

# Mid Game

Pressure side lanes.

Destroy outer towers.

Control vision around objectives.

Rotate early with wave priority.

---

# Late Game

Stay far behind your frontline.

Poke before committing.

Use Mega Inferno Bomb (R) to influence grouped fights.

Continue prioritizing towers and inhibitors whenever possible.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Fighting too close to enemies.
- Wasting Satchel Charge.
- Ignoring tower opportunities.
- Missing skillshots under pressure.
- Chasing kills instead of objectives.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Wave management.
- Skillshot accuracy.
- Tower damage.
- Objective participation.
- Positioning.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Ziggs players generally:

- Maintain excellent wave control.
- Consistently pressure towers.
- Land impactful Mega Inferno Bombs.
- Stay alive through objective fights.
- Convert lane pressure into structures.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 3

- Strong poke and wave control.

Level 6

- Mega Inferno Bomb provides global teamfight impact.

One Item

- Reliable wave clear and poke.

Two Items

- Excellent siege potential.

Three Items

- Elite late-game artillery capable of controlling objectives from long range.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Am I positioned outside enemy engage range?
- Can I safely poke before the fight begins?
- Is Satchel Charge available for escape or tower execution?
- Is Mega Inferno Bomb available?
- Can we take a tower if we win this fight?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Maintain wave priority before every Dragon.
- Deal meaningful poke before every major objective fight.
- Destroy the first bot tower whenever possible.
- Stay alive through every Baron and Dragon fight.
