# Caitlyn

## Champion Identity

Caitlyn is a lane-dominant marksman who excels at creating early gold advantages through superior range, wave control, and turret pressure.

She should aim to build an early lead and convert it into map control before hypercarries outscale her.

---

# Strengths

- Longest base attack range among ADCs.
- Excellent lane bullying.
- Strong turret siege.
- Safe farming.
- Strong objective control with traps.
- Powerful headshot trades.
- Strong early and late game.

---

# Weaknesses

- Limited mobility.
- Relies on positioning.
- Falls behind if early lead is lost.
- Struggles mid-game.
- Vulnerable to hard engage.
- Trap placement requires planning.

---

# Win Condition

Create a gold lead during lane.

Take the first bot turret.

Control neutral objectives through lane priority.

End games before late-game hypercarries fully scale.

---

# Lose Condition

Even lane against scaling ADCs.

Poor trap usage.

Repeated deaths to engage supports or junglers.

Falling behind in tempo.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Abuse attack range.
- Push for level advantages.
- Control bushes.
- Harass during enemy last hits.
- Crash waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Level 13 power spike.

Rotate after taking bot turret.

Pressure side lanes safely.

Control choke points with traps.

Avoid unnecessary isolation.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Protect positioning.

Use traps defensively around objectives.

Prioritize consistent DPS over flashy plays.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Constantly auto-pushing.
- Ignoring jungle pressure.
- Random trap placement.
- Using E aggressively without vision.
- Chasing kills.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Lane dominance.
- Gold lead at 15.
- Tower pressure.
- Trap efficiency.
- Positioning.
- Recall timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Caitlyn players generally:

- Maintain high CS.
- Generate early turret damage.
- Create lane priority consistently.
- Die very rarely before first tower falls.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Take first turret in winning lanes.
- Place traps before every dragon fight.
- Avoid dying before first objective.
