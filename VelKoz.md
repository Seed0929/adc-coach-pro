# Samira

Champion: Samira

Class: All-In Marksman

Difficulty: Very Hard

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Elite

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Physical Burst

Coach Priority:

- Engage Timing
- Combo Execution
- Positioning
- Target Selection

---

# Champion Identity

Samira is a high-risk, high-reward marksman who excels at cleaning up teamfights through explosive burst damage and resets. She is not a front-line engager. Instead, she waits patiently until enemy crowd control and major cooldowns have been used before committing.

A successful Samira player knows when NOT to fight.

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional teamfight carry potential.
- Powerful all-ins.
- High mobility.
- Strong reset mechanics.
- Excellent synergy with engage supports.
- Can quickly eliminate multiple enemies.

---

# Weaknesses

- Very short attack range.
- Extremely vulnerable to crowd control.
- Requires precise timing.
- Weak when behind.
- Punished heavily for poor engages.

---

# Win Condition

Survive the early game.

Build item advantages.

Wait for teammates to engage.

Enter fights after key enemy abilities have been used.

Use Inferno Trigger (R) to clean up fights.

---

# Lose Condition

Engaging first.

Using Ultimate while crowd control is available.

Fighting without support.

Overcommitting for kills.

Falling behind in lane.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade around support cooldowns.
- Farm safely until all-in opportunities appear.
- Respect enemy poke lanes.
- Avoid unnecessary extended trades.
- Push waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Look for skirmishes.

Play with teammates.

Avoid face-checking.

Fight around objectives where teammates can follow up.

---

# Late Game

Wait patiently before entering fights.

Attack the closest safe target until a clean engage appears.

Use Ultimate only when multiple enemies are vulnerable.

Prioritize survival over flashy plays.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Diving into fights first.
- Using Ultimate into available crowd control.
- Ignoring wave states.
- Chasing kills too deeply.
- Fighting while isolated.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Engage timing.
- Ultimate value.
- Death timing.
- Positioning.
- Kill participation.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Samira players generally:

- Wait for allies to engage.
- Use Ultimate after enemy crowd control has been spent.
- Stay alive until the middle of fights.
- Convert resets into objective control.
- Maintain strong farming despite aggressive play.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 2

- Strong all-in potential with support.

Level 6

- Inferno Trigger unlocks massive teamfight power.

One Item

- Reliable skirmishing.

Two Items

- Major carry potential.

Three Items

- Capable of taking over teamfights with proper timing.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Which enemy crowd control abilities are still available?
- Has my team already engaged?
- Can I safely stack Style before using Ultimate?
- Do I have an escape if the fight goes poorly?
- Is this a fight I need to commit to?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Never engage first in a teamfight.
- Use Inferno Trigger only after key crowd control has been used.
- Stay alive until at least 10 seconds into every major teamfight.
- Convert every successful teamfight into a Dragon, Baron, or tower.
