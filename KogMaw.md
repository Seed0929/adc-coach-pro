# Ashe

Champion: Ashe

Class: Utility Marksman

Difficulty: Easy

Lane Phase: Strong

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Very Low

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Vision
- Ultimate Usage
- Kiting

---

# Champion Identity

Ashe is a utility-focused marksman who provides consistent damage, exceptional vision control, and reliable crowd control. She wins games by enabling her team through superior information, engage potential, and safe sustained DPS.

---

# Strengths

- Reliable crowd control.
- Excellent vision with Hawkshot (E).
- Strong lane pressure.
- Consistent slows.
- Safe long-range engage with Enchanted Crystal Arrow (R).
- Excellent objective setup.

---

# Weaknesses

- No mobility.
- Extremely vulnerable to assassins.
- Requires strong positioning.
- Can struggle when behind.
- Relies on teammates to capitalize on engages.

---

# Win Condition

Maintain lane priority.

Provide vision before objectives.

Use Enchanted Crystal Arrow to create picks.

Stay alive while continuously slowing enemies during fights.

---

# Lose Condition

Getting caught without Flash.

Missing key Ultimate opportunities.

Ignoring vision responsibilities.

Standing too close to enemy engage champions.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Trade often using passive slows.
- Push when safe to gain priority.
- Harass during enemy last hits.
- Use Hawkshot to track the enemy jungler.
- Crash waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Scout objectives with Hawkshot.

Look for picks with Ultimate.

Rotate early for Dragons and Herald.

Support teammates through vision and utility.

---

# Late Game

Stay behind the frontline.

Attack the closest safe target.

Use Ultimate to begin or turn fights.

Maintain slows on priority threats.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Forgetting to use Hawkshot.
- Holding Ultimate for too long.
- Walking into engage range.
- Chasing kills.
- Fighting without vision.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Hawkshot usage.
- Ultimate accuracy and impact.
- Positioning.
- Vision contribution.
- Objective participation.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Ashe players generally:

- Use Hawkshot proactively every cooldown.
- Create picks with impactful Ultimates.
- Stay alive during objective fights.
- Provide consistent slows and DPS.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Use Hawkshot before every Dragon and Baron.
- Land at least one impactful Ultimate each game.
- Stay alive through every major teamfight.
- Maintain safe positioning behind your frontline.
