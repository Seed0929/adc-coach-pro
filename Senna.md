# Jinx

## Champion Identity

Jinx is a late-game hypercarry who scales into one of the highest sustained damage champions in League of Legends.

She sacrifices early lane strength for exceptional teamfight potential and objective damage.

Jinx wins by farming consistently, surviving fights, and taking over once she gets excited (Passive).

---

# Strengths

- Exceptional late-game DPS.
- Excellent objective damage.
- Long-range rocket autos.
- Strong teamfight resets.
- High tower damage.
- Powerful scaling.

---

# Weaknesses

- No mobility.
- Weak against hard engage.
- Vulnerable before items.
- Relies heavily on positioning.
- Easy to punish when caught.

---

# Win Condition

Farm efficiently.

Reach two to three completed items.

Stay alive during teamfights.

Trigger Passive resets to clean up fights.

---

# Lose Condition

Falling behind in farm.

Dying before fights begin.

Overextending without Flash.

Fighting aggressively before item spikes.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize CS over risky trades.
- Manage wave safely.
- Use rockets to secure difficult farm.
- Respect jungle pressure.
- Avoid unnecessary all-ins.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming side waves safely.

Rotate to objectives on time.

Avoid isolated fights.

Group when major objectives are spawning.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Attack the closest safe target.

Protect Flash.

Use rockets for maximum range.

Capitalize on Passive resets.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Fighting too early.
- Greedy positioning.
- Ignoring item spikes.
- Chasing kills after Passive activates.
- Missing farm while roaming.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- CS per minute.
- Death timing.
- Positioning in teamfights.
- Objective participation.
- Damage uptime.
- Farming consistency.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Jinx players generally:

- Average 8+ CS/min.
- Die very rarely before major objectives.
- Stay alive through most teamfights.
- Consistently deal high sustained damage.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Stay above 8 CS/min.
- Avoid dying before second Dragon.
- Focus the closest safe target in every teamfight.
