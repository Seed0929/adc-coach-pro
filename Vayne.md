# Nilah

Champion: Nilah

Class: Melee Skirmisher

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Average

Scaling: Excellent

Teamfighting: Excellent

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Sustained Physical

Coach Priority:

- Engage Timing
- Wave Management
- Positioning
- Teamfight Decision Making

---

# Champion Identity

Nilah is a melee ADC who thrives in extended fights through sustained damage, healing, and coordinated engages. She is strongest when paired with aggressive supports and excels at punishing poor positioning.

Nilah wins games by surviving difficult early lanes, reaching item spikes, and finding high-value multi-target engages.

---

# Strengths

- Elite scaling.
- Exceptional sustain.
- Strong all-in potential.
- Excellent teamfight Ultimate.
- High mobility.
- Strong against tank-heavy teams.

---

# Weaknesses

- Melee range.
- Vulnerable to poke lanes.
- Difficult early laning.
- Requires strong engage timing.
- Relies on teammates for setup.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Survive difficult matchups.

Reach core item spikes.

Find multi-target Ultimates in teamfights.

---

# Lose Condition

Taking unnecessary poke.

Fighting before item spikes.

Engaging alone.

Poor wave management.

Missing Ultimate opportunities.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Preserve health.
- Farm safely.
- Trade only when enemy cooldowns are unavailable.
- Coordinate with your support.
- Crash waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Fight around objectives.

Pressure side lanes safely.

Stay with teammates.

Look for coordinated engages.

---

# Late Game

Wait patiently before engaging.

Use Apotheosis (R) to hit multiple champions.

Prioritize survival over flashy plays.

Follow your frontline into fights.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Walking into poke unnecessarily.
- Fighting 1v2.
- Using Ultimate on a single low-value target.
- Ignoring wave states.
- Chasing kills without support.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- Farming consistency.
- Engage timing.
- Ultimate value.
- Teamfight positioning.
- Objective participation.
- Death timing.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Nilah players generally:

- Maintain 7.5–8 CS/min.
- Reach item spikes consistently.
- Land impactful multi-target Ultimates.
- Survive until the middle of teamfights.
- Convert winning fights into objectives.

---

# Power Spikes

Level 2

- Strong all-in potential with an engage support.

Level 6

- Apotheosis unlocks major teamfight impact.

One Item

- Reliable dueling and sustain.

Two Items

- Strong carry potential.

Three Items

- Elite late-game teamfighter capable of deciding games.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Have key enemy crowd control abilities been used?
- Can I reach the enemy carries safely?
- Will my Ultimate hit multiple champions?
- Is my support close enough to follow up?
- Can this fight lead to an objective?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Stay above 7.5 CS/min.
- Avoid unnecessary poke damage during lane.
- Land at least one multi-target Ultimate each game.
- Convert every successful teamfight into a Dragon, Baron, or tower.
