# Kog'Maw

Champion: Kog'Maw

Class: Hypercarry

Difficulty: Medium

Lane Phase: Weak

Scaling: Elite

Teamfighting: Elite

Wave Clear: Good

Mobility: Very Low

Primary Damage: Sustained Mixed Damage

Coach Priority:

- Positioning
- Farming
- Target Selection
- Teamfighting

---

# Champion Identity

Kog'Maw is a late-game hypercarry who excels at dealing overwhelming sustained damage from long range. His success depends on efficient farming, disciplined positioning, and maximizing Bio-Arcane Barrage (W) during extended fights.

Kog'Maw wins games by surviving long enough to become an unstoppable source of sustained DPS.

---

# Strengths

- Elite late-game scaling.
- Exceptional tank shredding.
- Long attack range with Bio-Arcane Barrage.
- Excellent objective damage.
- Massive sustained DPS.
- Strong front-to-back teamfighting.

---

# Weaknesses

- No mobility.
- Extremely vulnerable to assassins.
- Weak early game.
- Requires strong frontline protection.
- Punished heavily for positioning mistakes.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach two to three completed items.

Stay alive during teamfights.

Maximize Bio-Arcane Barrage uptime.

---

# Lose Condition

Falling behind in CS.

Getting caught before fights.

Overextending without protection.

Fighting before item spikes.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Prioritize farming.
- Avoid unnecessary trades.
- Respect engage lanes.
- Keep waves in safe positions.
- Recall on good wave timings.

---

# Mid Game

Continue farming efficiently.

Join objective fights.

Stay near teammates.

Avoid isolated skirmishes.

---

# Late Game

Fight front-to-back.

Activate Bio-Arcane Barrage before committing.

Attack the closest safe target.

Never sacrifice positioning for extra damage.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Walking too far forward.
- Fighting without frontline support.
- Ignoring farm for low-value fights.
- Activating Bio-Arcane Barrage too late.
- Chasing kills.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- CS per minute.
- Positioning.
- Bio-Arcane Barrage uptime.
- Teamfight survival.
- Damage uptime.
- Objective participation.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Kog'Maw players generally:

- Maintain 8+ CS/min.
- Stay alive through most teamfights.
- Maximize Bio-Arcane Barrage usage.
- Attack the closest safe target consistently.
- Convert objective fights into wins.

---

Level 6

- Living Artillery provides additional poke and vision.

One Item

- Farming and sustained damage improve significantly.

Two Items

- Major carry potential.

Three Items

- Elite hypercarry capable of melting both tanks and squishy champions.

---

# Threat Checklist

Before every fight ask:

- Is Bio-Arcane Barrage available?
- Which enemy champion can reach me first?
- Am I positioned behind my frontline?
- Can I attack safely without stepping forward?
- Do I have Flash available?

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Stay above 8 CS/min.
- Stay alive through every Dragon and Baron fight.
- Spend every teamfight attacking the closest safe target.
