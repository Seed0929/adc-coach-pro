# Ezreal

Champion: Ezreal

Class: Poke Marksman

Difficulty: Hard

Lane Phase: Good

Scaling: Good

Teamfighting: Good

Wave Clear: Average

Mobility: High

Primary Damage: Physical (Mixed Possible)

Coach Priority:

- Skillshot Accuracy
- Positioning
- Farming
- Cooldown Management

---

# Champion Identity

Ezreal is a safe, high-skill marksman who excels at dealing consistent poke damage while avoiding danger through superior positioning and mobility.

He wins by slowly creating health advantages before committing to fights.

Unlike traditional ADCs, Ezreal relies heavily on landing Mystic Shot (Q) to maximize damage and reduce cooldowns.

---

# Strengths

- Extremely safe with Arcane Shift (E).
- Strong poke throughout the game.
- Flexible itemization.
- Difficult to catch.
- Excellent objective control through sustained damage.
- Strong mid-game power spike.

---

# Weaknesses

- Lower sustained DPS than many hypercarries.
- Very skillshot dependent.
- Weak wave clear early.
- Easy to become irrelevant if Q accuracy is poor.
- Arcane Shift misuse is heavily punished.

---

# Win Condition

Farm consistently.

Reach first and second item power spikes.

Control fights with poke before committing.

Use Arcane Shift defensively whenever possible.

---

# Lose Condition

Missing too many Mystic Shots.

Using Arcane Shift aggressively without vision.

Poor farming.

Getting caught before objectives.

---

# Laning Priorities

- Farm safely.
- Poke with Mystic Shot.
- Conserve mana early.
- Respect engage supports.
- Crash waves before recalling.

---

# Mid Game

Pressure objectives with poke.

Stay grouped when needed.

Avoid unnecessary all-ins.

Maintain safe spacing.

---

# Late Game

Constantly poke before fights.

Attack the closest safe target.

Use Arcane Shift only when necessary.

Maintain high damage uptime.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Using Arcane Shift aggressively.
- Missing Mystic Shots consistently.
- Fighting before landing poke.
- Ignoring farm while looking for kills.
- Standing too close despite having long-range poke.

---

# Coaching Focus

BotDiff should evaluate:

- CS per minute.
- Skillshot consistency.
- Arcane Shift usage.
- Positioning.
- Death timing.
- Damage contribution.

---

# Mechanical Benchmarks

Strong Ezreal players generally:

- Maintain 7.5–8.5 CS/min.
- Use Arcane Shift primarily as a defensive tool.
- Land Mystic Shot consistently throughout fights.
- Stay alive while maintaining constant poke pressure.

---

# Practice Goals

Example goals:

- Reach 80 CS by 10 minutes.
- Use Arcane Shift defensively in every teamfight.
- Focus on landing Mystic Shot before committing to fights.
- Avoid deaths before major objectives.
